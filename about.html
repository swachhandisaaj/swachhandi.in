About us

Welcome to स्वच्छंदी SAAJ, We're dedicated to giving you the very best quality of product, with a focus on current fashion trends

Founded in 2018 by Miss. Rasika Medhe, स्वच्छंदी SAAJ has come a long way from its beginnings in a Karvenagar.When Rasika first started out, her passion for fashion designing,helping other people be more eco-friendly, providing the best outfits for his fellow customers drove her to do intense research and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming boutique.We now serve customers all over Pune and are thrilled to be a part of the fashionable wing of fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
